01 · Who we are

A quarter-century of presswork, delivered on time.

Milan Media Service is one of Bhubaneswar's longest-running printing presses — a specialist in bulk offset production for the institutions that keep Odisha running.

Since 2000, we've printed everything from examination booklets for state schools to tender documents for government departments, hospital forms, industrial labels, and corporate stationery. What hasn't changed in 25 years is our philosophy: every sheet leaves the press only when it meets our standard.

We run modern offset equipment, maintain strict quality control, and meet deadlines that matter — the kind that touch public services, patient care, and the academic calendar.
